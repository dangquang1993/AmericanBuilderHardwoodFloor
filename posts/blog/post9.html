Why White Oak is Our Top Recommendation

White oak consistently performs best in Quincy homes because:

  • Its closed grain structure resists moisture absorption
  • It's harder than red oak (better for active families)
  • The natural color variations hide minor scratches well
  • It takes stains beautifully for custom looks

Quincy's Climate and Your Hardwood Floors

Our local weather presents unique challenges for hardwood flooring:

Humidity Swings

Summer humidity averages 70%+ while winter heating drops indoor humidity below 30%. This causes wood to expand and contract.

Solution: Choose stable species like white oak and maintain 35-55% indoor humidity year-round.

Coastal Moisture

Ocean air carries salt moisture that can affect flooring near entryways.

Solution: Use water-resistant finishes and place quality mats at all entrances.

Winter Challenges

Snow, ice melt, and sand tracked inside can damage floors.

Solution: Install harder species like hickory in entryways and clean floors regularly.

Finish Options for Quincy Homes

The right finish protects your investment from our climate:

Best Finish Types

  • Aluminum Oxide UV-Cured: Most durable for active families
  • Water-Based Polyurethane: Low VOC, quick drying, good moisture resistance
  • Oil-Modified Polyurethane: Excellent for older homes with more humidity fluctuation

Recommended Sheen Levels

  • Satin (35% gloss): Our most popular - hides scratches well
  • Matte (20% gloss): Best for rustic looks and high-traffic areas
  • Semi-Gloss (55% gloss): Only recommended for formal spaces
