What's the difference between refinishing and sanding?
+

Sanding is part of the refinishing process. We sand down the old finish and surface imperfections, then apply new stain (if desired) and protective finish coats to restore your floors.

Can you match my existing hardwood floors?
+

Yes, we're experts at matching existing wood species, stain colors, and finishes. We'll bring samples to ensure a perfect match for additions or repairs.

Do you work with insurance companies?
+

Absolutely. We regularly work with insurance companies for water damage, fire damage, and other covered hardwood floor repairs. We can help with the claims process.

What type of finish do you recommend?
+

We typically recommend polyurethane finishes for their durability and protection. We offer both oil-based and water-based options, each with their own benefits:

  • Oil-based: Rich amber tone, extremely durable, longer drying time
  • Water-based: Clear finish, low odor, faster drying, more eco-friendly
How do I maintain my newly refinished floors?
+

Proper maintenance will keep your floors looking beautiful for years:

  • Use protective pads under furniture legs
  • Clean spills immediately to prevent water damage
  • Sweep or vacuum regularly with soft-bristle attachments
  • Use hardwood-specific cleaning products
  • Maintain consistent humidity levels (30-50%)
