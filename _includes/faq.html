Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common hardwood flooring questions

How long does hardwood floor refinishing take?

Most hardwood floor refinishing projects take 3-5 days depending on the size of the area and number of coats needed. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your free estimate.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! We provide completely free, no-obligation estimates for all hardwood floor services throughout the Greater Boston area. Call (617) 222-0001 to schedule yours.

What's the difference between refinishing and sanding?

Sanding is part of the refinishing process. We sand down the old finish and surface imperfections, then apply new stain (if desired) and protective finish coats to restore your floors.

Can you match my existing hardwood floors?

Yes, we're experts at matching existing wood species, stain colors, and finishes. We'll bring samples to ensure a perfect match for additions or repairs.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Absolutely. We regularly work with insurance companies for water damage, fire damage, and other covered hardwood floor repairs. We can help with the claims process.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Greater Boston including Boston, Cambridge, Newton, Quincy, Hingham, Braintree, Dedham, Needham, Norwood, Randolph, Dorchester, and Somerville. See our full service area.
